Interior Designing

Ohm Arch Architects & Planners is a full-service architecture studio based in Kakkanad, Kochi. The studio provides a wide range of services, from design to construction, and focuses on developing modern and contemporary designs for projects in the home, business, and public spaces. Thestudios design philosophy is based on the conviction that architecture should serve as an expression of a client&#39;s unique character and lifestyle, and that the design needs to take local context and culture into account. They make an effort to produce designs that adhere to ecological principles while also being aesthetically pleasing.

Designing

Design of Ohm Arch Architects & Planners design is a creative process used to create interior spaces for homes and businesses. In order to create a cohesive and aesthetically pleasing environment, itinvolves choosing the right materials, furniture, colours, textures, and lightingcomponents. It also covers the organisation and planning of the plumbing, electrical, and construction systems. The task of the interior designer is to reflect the needs, preferences, and style of the client while also ensuring that the space is functional for its users. The procedure typically entails conducting research, choosing materials, fixtures, and other components, as well as developing and putting into practice a design concept. To ensure the space functionality and safety, the designer also needs to be knowledgeable about regional building codes and regulations.
